What We Offer

Available Activities

From thrilling game drives to peaceful bird watching — there is an adventure waiting for every kind of visitor at Molefe Wild Trails. All activities are led by our expert, certified rangers.

Game Viewing

Experience the thrill of watching Africa's iconic animals in their natural habitat aboard our custom 4x4 game drive vehicles. Our expert rangers will guide you through the best spots and share fascinating knowledge about each animal you encounter along the way.

We offer both sunrise and sunset drives, each providing a completely different and equally breathtaking wildlife experience. Morning drives are perfect for spotting predators returning from a night hunt, while evening drives treat you to golden light and animals gathering at watering holes.

  • Sunrise and sunset drives available daily
  • Maximum 8 guests per vehicle for an intimate experience
  • Duration: 3 hours per drive
  • Suitable for all ages including families
  • Educational commentary by certified rangers

Hiking Trails

Explore the park's diverse and stunning landscapes on foot with our range of scenic hiking trails. Whether you prefer a leisurely family stroll or a challenging full-day hike, our trails wind through breathtaking bush, rocky kopjes, and lush riverine forest.

Every trail is guided by one of our experienced rangers who will help you identify animal tracks, bird calls, and fascinating plant life along the way. Walking through the bush gives you a completely different and more intimate connection with nature than a vehicle drive.

  • Easy Trail: 3km | 1.5 hours | Flat terrain
  • Moderate Trail: 8km | 4 hours | Some hills
  • Challenging Trail: 15km | Full day | Rugged terrain
  • All trails are guided by certified rangers for safety
  • Water and snacks provided on full day hikes

Bird Watching

Molefe Wild Trails is a certified Important Bird Area with over 300 recorded species. Our specialist birding guides will help you spot and identify everything from tiny weavers and colourful bee-eaters to majestic raptors and rare ground hornbills.

Early morning sessions are the most rewarding as birds are most active at dawn. We provide quality binoculars and illustrated field guides so you can identify every species you spot. Our dedicated bird hides at key watering points offer incredible close-up views without disturbing the birds.

  • Early morning sessions for best bird activity
  • Quality binoculars and field guides provided
  • Dedicated bird hides at watering points
  • Perfect for researchers, students and enthusiasts
  • Personalised species checklist provided

Guided Tours

Our full-day guided tours are the ultimate Molefe Wild Trails experience. Combining a morning game drive, a guided bush walk, lunch at our scenic bush camp, and an afternoon of bird watching — this tour gives you the most comprehensive experience of everything our park has to offer.

Perfect for first-time visitors, school excursions, and corporate groups. Our passionate rangers bring the bush to life with fascinating stories, wildlife tracking demonstrations, and in-depth knowledge of Botswana's ecology and conservation efforts.

  • Full day experience: 7:00AM – 5:00PM
  • Lunch at our scenic bush camp included
  • Educational wildlife talks and tracking lessons
  • Certificate of visit for school groups
  • Dedicated photo stops at scenic viewpoints
