Who We Are

Our Story

Molefe Wild Trails was founded in 2004 by a passionate family of conservationists who believed that Botswana's incredible wildlife deserved to be shared with the world responsibly, respectfully, and sustainably.

What started as a small, family-run game walk has grown into one of Botswana's most respected wildlife tourism destinations. Over the past two decades, we have welcomed thousands of visitors from curious schoolchildren to international wildlife researchers,all united by a love of nature.

Today, our team of expert rangers, conservationists, and hospitality professionals work together every single day to ensure every visit to Molefe Wild Trails is safe, educational, and truly unforgettable.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do at Molefe Wild Trails every single day.

Conservation

Protecting Botswana's wildlife and natural habitats for future generations is at the very core of all we do.

Education

We believe knowledge creates stewards of the land. Every visit to our park is a meaningful learning opportunity.

Community

We partner with and uplift local communities, employing local staff and supporting local businesses and suppliers.

Excellence

We set the highest standards in visitor safety, service quality, and the care and welfare of our wildlife.
